Projects

Project One:

Happy Skin Planner

Description:

Users can create a customized weekly skin planner to organize their daily skin regime. They can add the product name, designate whether to be scheduled for morning or evening use, and mark the days for product use. When submitted, a weekly schedule is set up with added products and use may mark them as completed or reset the button to mark as incomplete. User may also delete specific products that would simultaneously delete the product from their weekly schedule.

Project Three:

Petful FIFO

Description:

This project was created with the intent of pairing a pet with a new home. Petful FIFO works in a First in First out basis and matches prospective owners with the cat or dog that has been in the shelter longest and is next in line. By adding your name, you will be added to the list of people in line, and will be allowed to choose either a cat or dog to adopt when you reach the front of the line.
